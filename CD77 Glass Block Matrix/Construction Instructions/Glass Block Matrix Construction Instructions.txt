Glass Block Matrix Construction Instructions
By
Chemdoc77

1. Gather needed materials.  See glass block 1 picture and bill of materials
2. Put matrix in gallon freezer bag and cut off the top of the freezer bag including the Ziplock part at the top of the freezer bag.  See glass block 2 picture
3. Carefully fold matrix in the freezer bag and place it in the glass block – see glass block picture 3
4. After unfolding the matrix in the freezer bag and with it firmly against the back of the glass block, slowly add some of the fire pit glass.  Use a long screwdriver or spatula to pack the fire pit glass tightly together so that you cannot see any of the matrix when looking at the glass block from the front. See glass block picture 4.
5. Add in stages enough fire pit glass to completely fill the area on front of the matrix with the fire pit glass. See glass block picture 5.
